Elemental Mastery

Elemental Mastery is a third person shooter where the combination of different elements is key to progressing through the world.

Elemental Mastery is set in an ancient world where the elements are the prime rulers. The player will be able to harness these elements to complete puzzles,

defeat enemies and find objects to aid them throughout the game. The player has access to five different elements which include Earth, Fire, Water, Ice and Lightning.

With the elements at hand, the player will be able to upgrade their elemental abilities further through the exploration of the world and the many secrets it holds.

Currently, each element has four different abilities which consist of a left click/ primary fire, and three others

ranging from damaging abilities, utility, movement or healing.

Earth

First Ability: Rock Throw - By aiming in with right click and pressing left click, the player will throw a rock which damages enemies.

Second Ability: Rock Pillar - By pressing '1', a pillar will rise from the ground putting the player in a higher position.

Third Ability: Rock Wall - By aiming in with right click and pressing '2', a wall will be created in front of the player to provide cover.

Fourth Ability: Earthquake - Creates an earthquake zone around the player which does heavy damage to enemies that are on the ground.

Fire

First Ability: Sparks - By aiming in with right click and holding left click, the player will shoot out flame sparks to damage enemies.

Second Ability: Flame Walk - By pressing '1', the player moves forward and leaves a flame trail behind them that damages enemies. Needs to be improved.

Third Ability: Fireball - By aiming in with right click and pressing '3', a fireball will be shot out which damages enemies.

Fourth Ability: Fire Pistol - By aiming in with right click and holding '4', small fire bullets are shot out in front of the player and damages enemies

Water

First Ability: Waterball - By aiming in with right click and pressing left click, the player will shoot out a water ball which damages enemies.

Second Ability: Water Shield - By pressing '1', the player is coated by a water shield which makes them invincible but they can't use any other ability.

Third Ability: Healing Rain - By aiming in with right click and pressing '2', the player will create a rain cloud where the rain will start to heal them.

Fourth Ability: Tidal Wave - By aiming in with right click and pressing '3', the player will create a giant wave which pushes enemies and damages them.
