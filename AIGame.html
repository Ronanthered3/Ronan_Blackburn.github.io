AI Coding

Within a subject a QUT, I learnt how to code AI using both finite state machines and behaviour trees.

In the Gif above, my AI bot was apart of an end of semester tournament where I won third place out of 64 people.

The behaviour of the bot was simple as if it entered combat with an enemy, it would try to get very close where it

would unload all of it's weaponry at the opponent. If it was low and out of combat, it would go towards a health pack.

Alongside the behaviour tree, I also completed an assignment where predator/prey behaviour was used.

Alongside that, the small spaceships also incorporated bee like behaviour with the use of boiding for flight paths.
